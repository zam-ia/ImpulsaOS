# GrowthBrain Local Product Brief

GrowthBrain Local is not a posting toy. It is an operating desk for a business owner who has one scarce asset: attention.

The product turns verified business knowledge into usable marketing output: ideas, copy, scripts, visual pieces, calendar slots, tracked WhatsApp intent, and follow-up prompts. It keeps the human in the risky parts: final approval, WhatsApp conversations, pricing decisions, and closing.

## Product Stance

Automation should remove repetition, not judgment. The app must make the next commercial action obvious without pretending every channel can be safely automated for free.

## Primary User

A solo operator or small-business owner who sells through WhatsApp, needs organic content, and cannot spend every day inventing posts from scratch.

## Non-Negotiables

- Every generated asset traces back to a product, price, offer, CTA, and QA state.
- Publishing can fall back to a manual checklist when Meta permissions are missing.
- WhatsApp stays protected: tracked links and suggested replies are allowed; unofficial automation and mass sends are not.
- The interface should feel like a workbench, not a landing page.

## Voice

Direct Spanish. Short labels. No hedging. Empty states should tell the user what to do next, not explain the entire feature.
